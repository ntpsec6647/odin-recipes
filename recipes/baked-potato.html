Baked Potato Recipe

A Staple Baked Potato

This is a simple dish to create a brilliant baked potato covered in cheese and beans.

Ingredients

  • Baking Potato
  • Mature Cheese
  • Baked Beans
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Bake the potato until crispy
  2. Heat baked beans
  3. Split open and cover inners with butter
  4. Layer on top Grated cheese and baked beans
  5. Fin
