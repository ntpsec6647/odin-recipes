Lasagna Recipe

A Hearty Italian Lasagna

This is a hearty dish of lasagna sheets filled with the best british beef and covered with delectable mature cheese.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Sheets
  • Mature Cheese
  • British Beef
  • Red Sauce
  • Cream Sauce

Steps

  1. Cook Beef
  2. Add and mix red sauce
  3. Layer Beef, Lasagna Sheet, Cream Sauce until finished
  4. Cover with Cheese
  5. Bake
